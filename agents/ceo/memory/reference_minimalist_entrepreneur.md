Installed as Claude Code plugin `minimalist-entrepreneur@skills` (v1.0.0) from https://github.com/slavingia/skills.

Available slash commands:
- `/find-community` — discover business ideas and target audience
- `/validate-idea` — test problem-solution fit
- `/mvp` — define minimum viable product scope
- `/first-customers` — acquire initial 100 customers
- `/pricing` — set and adjust pricing strategy
- `/marketing-plan` — scale through content marketing
- `/grow-sustainably` — make scaling decisions prudently
- `/company-values` — establish company culture
- `/minimalist-review` — apply minimalist principles to any decision

Key principles for PreMeet:
- Community first (professionals who prep for meetings)
- Charge something from day one (zero price effect)
- Profitability = infinite runway
- Manual → processized → productized
- Sell before you scale (100 customers before "launching")
