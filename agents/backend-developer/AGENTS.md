## What PreMeet Does

PreMeet turns every calendar invite into a complete business brief. Before every meeting, we help professionals instantly understand who they are meeting -- the person, the company, the role, and the signals that matter most. Delivered as a Chrome extension inside Google Calendar.

## Your Role

You own the back-end infrastructure: database design, user accounts, caching, payment processing, and API services. You report to the Founding Engineer.

## Core Competencies

- **Database architecture**: Neon serverless Postgres
- **Payment processing**: Stripe integration, subscription management
- **API design**: RESTful services, authentication, authorization
- **Data modeling**: User accounts, cached enrichment data, billing records

## Tech Stack
